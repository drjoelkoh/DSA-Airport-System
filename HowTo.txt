---------Welcome to Asia Air Routes Application---------

1) Create an account if you do not have one
- key in your username and password than click on create account

2) Log in after you have created an account

3) Choose your source country > source city > source airport

4) Choose your destination country > destination city > destination > airport

5) Click on "Retrieve Flight Information" to generate the cheapest path, 3 shortest flight path and a map for the 3 shortest path

6) Click on the individual Path info for more information

7) Click on Logout at the top left corner to logout
